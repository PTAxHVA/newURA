About

Assoc. Prof. Quan Thanh Tho is an Associate Professor at HCMUT and the founder and pioneering leader of the Unlimited Research Group of AI (URA). He received his B.Eng. in Information Technology from HCMUT (1998) and his Ph.D. from Nanyang Technological University (2006). As Dean of the Faculty of Computer Science and Engineering, his relentless pursuit of innovation and high-impact research continues to inspire the next generation of scholars and practitioners in computer science.

His research spans artificial intelligence, natural language processing, intelligent systems, and formal methods. As of 2024, he has published 213 scholarly works in leading international conferences and journals and has accumulated 2,538 citations on Google Scholar, reflecting the global impact of his research. In 2024, he was honored with the prestigious “Outstanding Scientist with International Publications” award from Vietnam National University – Ho Chi Minh City (VNU-HCM). In the same year, he authored 21 international publications, including 5 high-impact Q1 journal articles (3 in top-20 venues) with a combined Impact Factor of 32.956, and 5 A-ranked conference papers at globally renowned conferences.
